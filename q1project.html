Padre Damaso

  • The real father of Maria Clara was Padre Damaso, a former town curate in San Diego. The man attempted to keep his daughter and Crisostomo Ibarra apart for Maria Clara's benefit by opposing their marriage..

Crisostomo Ibarra

  • Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, also known as Crisostomo Ibarra, was a young college student from San Diego. After his father passed away, he went back to his hometown to try to start a proper school, but he encountered many challenges from the locals. After being implicated in a rebellion, he later left the town and assumed the name Simoun, a jeweler.
