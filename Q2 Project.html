"The Chocolate Hills"

is a one-of-a-kind geological structure consisting of over 1000 cone-shaped hills that change color depending on the season.

The Underground River

The underground river is the longest navigable river in the world, with a unique karst ecosystem that is home to endangered and indigenous flora and animals. It is a must-see tourist site.

Vigan City

Vigan, one of the Philippines' oldest towns, is a Spanish Colonial fairy tale with dark-wood palaces, cobblestone lanes, and clattering kalesa (horse-drawn carriages).
